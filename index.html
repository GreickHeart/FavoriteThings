My Favorite Animal

The Spotted Hyena

Most people are quick to dismiss hyenas. They aren't as majestic looking as lions or as impressive as elephants. But they are fearsome predators.

  • Hyena's have a bite force of 1,100 psi, much stronger than Lions or Tigers. Those jaws are perfect for crunching through bone.
  • Hyena's are more closely related to cats than dogs!
  • The Spotted Hyena can run at 37mph.
